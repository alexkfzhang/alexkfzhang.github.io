Auto-Encoding Adversarial Imitation Learning
Kaifeng Zhang, Rui Zhao, Ziming Zhang & Yang Gao
International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR), 2022, in Submission
arXiv / code & data / blog

Summary:
(1) We propose to use auto-encoder based reward function formulation for inverse RL which is the first to the best of our knowledge.
(2) We find the newly proposed auto-encoding IRL provides more informative learning signals compared to the discriminator based methods.
(3) It can also denoise the expert demonstrations since the encoding-decoding process.
(4) Empircally, our method achieves 16.1% and 46.5% relative improvement overall compared to the best baseline FAIRL and PWIL on clean and noisy expert data respectively.

A Generative Adversarial Nets based Intelligent Self Calibration Method for EPS in Cars
Kaifeng Zhang
PCT Patent PCT/CN2021/093935
Work Done at Huawei.
This patent introduce a novel generative adversarial nets based approach to clibrate the steering systems of the cars (EPS). We combine trajectory matching, GANs, and a pre-trained neural network to learn the parameters of the EPS system with the aim of making the steering feeling similar to some calibrated cars.

Publications

On Mode Collapse in Generative Adversarial Networks
Kaifeng Zhang
International Conference on Artificial Neural Networks (ICANN), 2021 (Oral)
GANs with supervision signal (SSGAN) introduces a supervision signal to alleviate the mode collapse issue in training GANs. The supervision signal tells the generator an approximate output corresponding to the input noise, which ensures the generated manifold to be close to the data manifold. Therefore, the generator could be able to better capture the whole data distribution.

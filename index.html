Auto-Encoding Adversarial Imitation Learning
Kaifeng Zhang, Rui Zhao, Ziming Zhang & Yang Gao
arXiv / code /

Summary:
(1) To the best of our knowledge, we propose the first formulation of auto-encoder based reward signal for adversarial imitation learning.
(2) Our auto-encoding AIL provides a denser reward signal to the agent policy.
(3) Our method achieves 16.4% and 47.2% relative improvement overall compared to the best baseline FAIRL and PWIL on clean and noisy expert data, respectively.

On Mode Collapse in Generative Adversarial Networks
Kaifeng Zhang
International Conference on Artifical Neural Networks (ICANN), 2021
Oral Presentation
PDF

Summary:
(1) We propose GANs with supervision signal which introduces a supervision signal to alleviate the mode collapse in training GANs.

A Generative Adversarial Nets based Intelligent Self Calibration Method for EPS System in Cars
Kaifeng Zhang
PCT Patent PCT/CN2021/093935
Work Done at Huawei.
This patent introduce a novel generative adversarial nets based approach to clibrate the steering systems of the cars (EPS). We combine trajectory matching, GANs, and a pre-trained neural network to learn the parameters of the EPS system with the aim of making the steering feeling similar to some calibrated cars.
